VI explores the question of how individuals fight for a cause they support. It was through her love for nature that Coralie began to wonder how to protect it, and how others deal with this problem. Once she began to actively engage herself in this struggle, she quickly realized the thin line between what is right and what is not. And how one may attempt to judge it.
